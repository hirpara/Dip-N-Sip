Revolutionizing the way you Sip!


The different flavoured amazing straws from Dip and Sip are fun & great way to sip your water, a product that both parents, kids and youngsters alike love!

Drinking water through the different flavoured straws mean there is no mess as the flavoured beads stay in the straw allowing the water to flow through & create the amazing refreshing feeling and experience. It is so simple to use, just open the packet and place the straw in the water and enjoy.
